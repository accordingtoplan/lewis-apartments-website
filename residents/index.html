Common questions

Resident FAQs.

Log in to the resident portal at residentportal.lewisapartments.com to pay rent by bank transfer or card. Payments made before 5pm PT on a business day are typically posted the same day.
We commit to responding to all maintenance requests within one business day. Emergency maintenance (no heat, water leaks, locked out) is responded to 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Some restrictions apply — see your leasing office for full details.
The easiest way is through the resident portal — you can submit a request, add photos and track its status. You can also contact your community's management office directly by phone or email.
We'll contact you 90 days before your lease expiry with renewal options. You can accept, request different terms, or let us know you'll be moving out — all through the resident portal or by contacting your management office.
Pet policies vary by community. Most communities are pet-friendly with breed and weight restrictions. Contact your community office for specific policies, pet fees and deposits that apply to your home.
Most communities require 30 days written notice. Please check your lease agreement for your specific notice period, then submit your notice through the resident portal or in writing to your management office.

Our promise to you

The Lewis 24-Hour Service Commitment.

When something in your home needs attention, we take it seriously.

We commit to responding to every maintenance request within one business day. Emergency issues are addressed 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — because your home should always work the way it should.
