Who we are

Better communities.
Better lives.

Lewis Apartment Communities is part of the Lewis Group of Companies — a family-led community development group founded in 1955 by Goldy and Ralph Lewis. What began as a homebuilding company in Southern California has grown into one of the nation's largest privately held community developers, with a legacy that includes more than 58,000 homes, 13,000 apartments, and 24 million square feet of retail, office and industrial space throughout California and Nevada.


We take a community-first approach to development. We are devoted to leaving the world better than we found it — by building meaningful relationships, fostering a culture of inclusion and empowerment, creating new opportunities, and delivering real long-term value for residents and the wider communities we serve.
